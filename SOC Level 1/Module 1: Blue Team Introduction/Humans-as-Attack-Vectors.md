# Humans as Attack Vectors

## Learning Objectives
- Understand why humans are common attack targets.
- Learn common social engineering techniques.
- Recognize the SOC's role in detecting and mitigating human-focused attacks.

## Notes
- Humans are often the weakest link in cyber security.
- **Social engineering** manipulates people through trust, urgency, fear, or curiosity.
- Common attacks include:
  - Phishing emails
  - Malware downloads
  - Deepfakes
  - Impersonation
- Mitigation methods include:
  - Anti-phishing tools
  - Antivirus/EDR
  - Security awareness training
  - "Trust but verify" practices
- The **SOC** investigates attacks that bypass preventative security controls.
- SOC analysts also recommend security improvements and educate users.
